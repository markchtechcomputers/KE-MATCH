About KE MATCH

KE MATCH is a modern dating platform built for genuine connections. Our goal is to bring together people from across Kenya (and beyond) in a safe, fun, and simple way.

Why KE MATCH?

  • Simple Login → Sign up quickly using your Google or Facebook account.
  • Smart Profiles → Share who you are, what you love, and what you're looking for.
  • Fair Access → Everyone gets 10 free profiles to view per day.
  • Affordable Activation → Unlock unlimited chatting and contact sharing for just Ksh 150.
  • Safe & Secure → Verified accounts, report & block options keep your experience safe.

KE MATCH is more than a dating app — it's a place to meet new people, build friendships, and maybe even find love. ❤️
